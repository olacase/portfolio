About Me

Goal driven, reserved and knowledge savvy, willing to travel. Career Goal (span of 5years) To work with a proactive and effervescent strategic management team in any performance and competition driven organization, where emphasis is not on monotonous repetitive tasks that stifle creativity but where continuous improvement is both a culture and an imperative. Career Objective (span of 10 years) To emerge as the Head of a strategic business unit of such an organization with a leverage to initiate or contribute to strategic change and improve people and process management in the organization, with a view to help the attainment or sustenance of competitive advantage. Inspiration The real product of any company or organization regardless of its industry is essentially the quality of its thinking. The economy of the world will be driven by creative thinking (knowledge) and much less by technology, competition would go to new height and the ability of an organization to manage and cope with change will be a function of its investment in keen minds in this decade and beyond. Philosophy I can do what you cannot do you can do what I cannot do; together we can do great things.
